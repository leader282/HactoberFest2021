Hello, Welcome to the Shooting Game!!

Rules for playing the game:

  • Select the difficulty level to play the game
  • Click start to start the game
  • Click reset to reset the highest score
  • Click quit to quit the game
  • Drag the mouse to choose the direction and the velocity of
    shoot. The dotted line represents the path to be followed by the
    ball.
  • The difficulty levels are based on the relative accuracy of
    shooting. Level easy allows even crude shoots to count towards
    a new point whereas level hard allows only fine shoots.
